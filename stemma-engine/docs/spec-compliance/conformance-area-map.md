# Conformance Area Map

Canonical registry for the stemma Word-compliance audit. Built by deduping, normalizing, and ranking 70 candidate areas down to **69** areas. Source of truth: `reference/derived/conformance-areas.json` (each entry carries `topics`, `grep`, `refFiles`, and `rationale`; this file is the human-readable index).

Counts by priority: **39 high · 22 medium · 8 low**.

Ranking rule: high to low by expected findings. Dark corners, low existing coverage, and high interaction/precedence/tracked-change risk rank high; mature, well-covered basics rank low. Order is stable within a priority band.

## High priority (39)

Low or near-zero existing coverage combined with high divergence risk: bitmask/precedence resolution (conditional formatting, toggle XOR on the run side), tracked-change accept/reject corners (move, property-change, numbering, table-structure revisions), multi-hop resolution (numbering style links, theme fonts, OPC relationships), and entirely-unprobed structured/opaque constructs (form fields, SDT typed sub-tags, custom XML, MCE AlternateContent, font embedding, altChunk).

## Intentionally excluded

- **SpreadsheetML and PresentationML (ISO/IEC 29500 / ECMA-376 Parts covering xlsx/pptx).** stemma is a WordprocessingML (DOCX) engine. Spreadsheet/presentation parts, their content types, and their relationships are out of scope.
- **DrawingML / chart / diagram semantics (§20, §21, charts, SmartArt).** `w:drawing` and embedded objects are handled as opaque preserved blobs with relationship-integrity checks (see `runcontent-embedded-objects-ruby`). The internal DrawingML/chart element trees are not semantically manipulated, so element-level conformance of those trees is excluded; only round-trip preservation and r:id integrity are in scope.
- **VML legacy graphics internals.** Preserved opaquely (and as MCE Fallback content, see `mce-roundtrip-preservation`); the VML element model itself is not a conformance target.
- **Macro / VBA project parts (vbaProject.bin) and OLE binary internals.** Preserved as binary parts with relationship integrity only; no semantic parsing.
- **Rendering/layout fidelity (pagination, line breaking, font metrics).** stemma does not render; layout-only properties are validated for round-trip and enum/range correctness, not for visual output. Checking against real Word covers open-clean and accept/reject behavior, not pixel layout.
- **Encryption, DRM, and digital signatures (Part 2 signature/encryption packages).** Disallowed/unsupported package features are rejected (see `opc-zip-physical-package`); their internal markup is not a conformance target.
- **Web settings, frameset, and HTML round-trip parts.** Legacy Word-to-HTML constructs are preserved opaquely if present, not probed semantically.

## Method notes

- Dedup: merged `cnfStyle-gating` + `table-styles-conditional-cascade` into `table-conditional-formatting-cnf`; split `CT_OnOff` value-domain out of the pagination/misc candidates into its own `onoff-boolean-semantics` area; folded altChunk/subDoc into `alt-format-import-altchunk-subdoc`; kept `run-fonts-complex-script` and `theme-fonts-rfonts-resolution` separate (distinct rulesets: codepoint slot selection vs theme-token resolution); kept the two MCE areas (resolution vs round-trip preservation) and the two OMML areas (structure vs tracked-change) separate as distinct test families; renamed the hierarchy-level toggle area to `toggle-properties-xor-hierarchy` (low) distinct from the run-element `run-toggle-properties` (high).
- Path fix: the candidate `isLgl` reference used prefix `196_`; the actual file is `197_17.9.4-islgl-...`, corrected in `lvltext-numfmt-rendering`. All other 369 candidate paths verified present.
